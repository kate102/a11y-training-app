Alt Text: Say Enough

Adding alternative text for images is the first principle of web accessibility.

It is also one of the most difficult to properly implement.

Alt text should be

  • Specific
  • Accurate
  • Unambigous

Context is everything.

  • Hydra is a steel Floorless Coaster at Dorney Park & Wildwater Kingdom.
  • Hydra is the largest of the 88 modern constellations.
  • Hydra is one of the Saronic Islands of Greece.
  • Hydra is a many headed moster in Greek mythology.
